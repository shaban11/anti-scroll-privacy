Privacy Policy for Anti-Scroll

Last updated: December 20, 2024

Our Commitment

Anti-Scroll is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

Anti-Scroll does NOT collect any personal information. All app usage data is processed locally on your device and never leaves your phone.

Permissions Explained

  • Usage Access: Required to monitor app usage times locally on your device
  • Display Over Other Apps: Used to show timer notifications when limits are reached
  • Notifications: To display timer status in your notification bar

All permissions are used solely for app functionality and no data is transmitted.

Data Storage

  • All settings and timer data are stored locally on your device
  • No cloud storage or external servers are used
  • All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Anti-Scroll does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for personal productivity and is not intended for monitoring others. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
